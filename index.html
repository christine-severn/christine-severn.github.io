Hello Yall!

Welcome to my personal page! Here I may share things associated with my academic, educational, and personal life!

  • I am currently an adjunct instructor for the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley located in Edinburg, Texas. I have been working there since 2018 where I also previously held positions as a Graduate and Teaching Assistant.
  • I have been married to my spouse for going on 13 years! We have a teenage child who is currently in middle school and participates in UIL Science competitions and has recently joined the McAllen Public Library's Teen Advisory Committee (T.A.C.) that helps create and put on programs for the library's teen department.
  • We are fur parents to seven cats! We had three cats originally until an outside cat decided to make herself at home. She ended up having three kittens, which brings our cat total to seven! But we love them all and their differing personalities.

That's just a little bit about myself! Take a look around my website to learn more about me and my life! Enjoy!

Academic Links

Academic Links

The following are links to organizations and networks that I am affiliated with.

UTRGV

UTRGV Home Page

I have been employed by The University of Texas Rio Grande Valley as an adjunct instructor since 2018. Previous positions at UTRGV include: Student Mentor, Graduate Assistant, and Teaching Assistant.

Sigma Tau Delta

Sigma Tau Delta Home Page

Sigma Tau Delta is an international English Honor Society. I was appointed President of the UTRGV chapter of Sigma Tau Delta during both my Undergrad and Graduate programs.

Texas Library Association (TLA)

Texas Library Association Home Page

As a current student in an MLIS program, I am able to join the TLA as a student member with reduced membership fees!

English MA Thesis

Check Out My MA Thesis!

I hold an MA in English Literature with a concentration in mental health and cultural studies. My MA Thesis is titled: Representations of Mental Health in Young Adult Literature: A Cultural Analysis of the Three Ps of Patient, Practitioner, and Population.

UTRGV Department of Literatures and Cultural Studies

UTRGV LCS Home Page

UTRGV's English department is divided into two separate areas of study: Language and Writing Studies and Literatures and Cultural Studies. I am a part of the department of Literatures and Cultural Studies where I teach various English Literature courses as well as a specialized alternative to College Rhetoric and Writing: ENGL1305 - Writing Cultural Studies. This course uses cultural texts to analyze and interpret various literary works.
